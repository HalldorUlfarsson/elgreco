## Catalogue of Halldorophones

This catalogue excludes early experiments that explored different instrument typologies as a platform for the feedback-string method.

Maintained by Halldór Úlfarsson. Last updated 10 May 2025. Corrections or additions: [halldorophone@gmail.com](mailto:halldorophone@gmail.com)

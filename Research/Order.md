<h1 class="order-heading">Order of the Halldorophone</h1>

<img src="../assets/img/Order.jpg" alt="Belt of the Order of the Halldorophone" class="order-img" />

<p class="order-intro-text">Unique leather belts featuring the halldorophone logo on an anodised aluminium buckle made by Halldór Úlfarsson are occasionally awarded to persons who have contributed in extraordinary ways to the evolution of halldorophones.</p>

<h2 class="order-subheading">Holders of the Belt</h2>

<p class="order-award-note">In order of award</p>

<div class="order-entry">
<h3>Halldór Úlfarsson</h3>
<p>For inventing halldorophones</p>
</div>

<div class="order-entry">
<h3>Arngrímur Guðmundsson</h3>
<p>For his exceptional contribution to the development of halldorophones</p>
</div>

<div class="order-entry">
<h3>Hildur Guðnadóttir</h3>
<p>For making halldorophones infamous</p>
</div>

<div class="order-entry">
<h3>Davíð Brynjar Franzson</h3>
<p>For selfless service in bringing halldorophones to America</p>
</div>

<div class="order-entry">
<h3>Thor Magnusson</h3>
<p>For his wisdom and tutelage of Halldór Úlfarsson</p>
</div>
